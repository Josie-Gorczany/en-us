Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up via TréZor.io/Stárt

Setting up your TréZor device is a seamless process when you follow the official instructions provided at TréZor.io/Stárt. Here's how to get started:

1. Unbox Your TréZor Device

Ensure that your TréZor package includes:

  • The TréZor device (Model One, Model T, Safe 3, or Safe 5)

  • USB connecting cable

  • Recovery seed cards

  • User manual TréZor

Inspect the packaging for any signs of tampering. If you notice anything suspicious, contact TréZor Support immediately. 

2. Navigate to TréZor.io/Stárt

Using a secure browser, go to TréZor.io/Stárt. This official page provides comprehensive instructions tailored to your specific TréZor model. 

3. Install TréZor Suite

Download and install the TréZor Suite application compatible with your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux). This software serves as the interface for managing your TréZor wallet.

4. Connect and Initialize Your Device

Connect your TréZor device to your computer using the USB cable. The TréZor Suite will prompt you to install the latest firmware. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete this step. 

5. Create a New Wallet

After installing the firmware, select the option to "Create new wallet." This process will generate a unique recovery seed (also known as a wallet backup), which is crucial for accessing your funds if your device is lost or damaged.

6. Secure Your Recovery Seed

Write down the 12, 18, or 24-word recovery seed on the provided card and store it in a secure, offline location. Never share this seed with anyone or store it digitally.

7. Set a PIN

Enhance your device's security by setting a unique PIN. This PIN will be required each time you access your TréZor wallet.

Tips for Maintaining Wallet Security

To ensure the ongoing security of your TréZor wallet:

  • Regular Firmware Updates: Keep your device's firmware up to date to protect against vulnerabilities. 

  • Beware of Phishing: Always verify that you're on the official TréZor website before entering sensitive information. 

  • Use Passphrase Protection: Add an extra layer of security by enabling passphrase protection in the TréZor Suite.

  • Secure Physical Storage: Store your TréZor device and recovery seed in separate, secure locations.
